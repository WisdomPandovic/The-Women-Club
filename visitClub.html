Location

Nestled in the heart of downtown Manhattan, The Women’s Club stands as an oasis overlooking Central Park. Surrounded by the energy of the city that never sleeps, members can enjoy the tranquility of the park and its lush greenery.

Address:Level 4, 123 Broadway Avenue, New York, NY, USA.

Facilities

The Club offers a warm, elegant yet informal ambience. Named after women who made significant contributions to Club and public life, our rooms can be reserved for private meetings, celebrations or receptions. All events are expertly managed by our experienced staff.

Events

We host a range of events including a monthly Club Lunch with a fine-dining seasonal menu. Weekly cultural programming including Women in Music, Women in Wine, Salon Series, and in-conversation events.

The Club’s Member Circles are an opportunity to pursue interests spanning technology, science, architecture, current affairs, literature, and more. Our Circles evolve and reflect our members’ passions, curiosity, and areas of expertise.
